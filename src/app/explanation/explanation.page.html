Passwords
Explanation:
Passwords are what keep your information safe, it's like putting a lock on your door. It's important to have a strong password, a good lock
A good password makes it more difficult for anyone to breka into anything you're keeping safe (i.e. profiles). Passwords consist of:
  • Passwords that are not reused
  • Is not easy to guess
  • a mix of characters (i.e. uppercase and lowercase text, numbers, and symbols
  • the longer the password the better
